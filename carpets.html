Loop Pile

Loop pile carpets are durable and hard-wearing, making them ideal for busy areas like stairs, landings and hallways. The textured finish adds a modern look while helping to hide footprints.

  • Great for high-traffic areas
  • Stylish textured appearance
  • Hides marks and footprints

Saxony, thick and fluffy

Saxony carpets are deep-pile and super soft underfoot, giving that luxury feel. They're perfect for bedrooms and living rooms where comfort is king.

  • Soft and cosy underfoot
  • Perfect for lounges and bedrooms
  • Adds a touch of luxury

Twist Pile, everyday durable

Twist pile carpets have tightly twisted fibres that give a firm feel underfoot. They're one of the most popular choices for family homes because they're tough, practical and easy to maintain.

  • Strong and long-lasting
  • Great for hallways, stairs and living rooms
  • Stain-resistant options available

Other carpet options

Looking for something a little different? We also supply Berber, velvet and plush, cut-and-loop designs, and natural fibre carpets such as wool, sisal and seagrass. Carpet tiles are another versatile option, perfect for offices, playrooms or anywhere that needs extra practicality. Whatever your style or budget, we can bring samples to your home so you can see the options in your own space.

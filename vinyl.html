Halls and living spaces

Vinyl is a great way to transform a hall into a bright, inviting space. It offers the look of real wood without the hefty price tag, and with its durability and easy maintenance it stays looking good for years.

Bathrooms

Frequently asked questions

Is vinyl good for bathrooms?

Yes, vinyl is waterproof and slip-resistant, with R10 and R11 ratings available for extra grip.

How is vinyl different from LVT?

Sheet vinyl is a soft, budget-friendly waterproof floor laid in large pieces; LVT is a premium tile that's glued down for a more solid, high-end finish.

Do you supply and fit?

Yes, we bring samples to your home, supply the vinyl and fit it for you.

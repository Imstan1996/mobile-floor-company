About Us

Your local, family-run flooring team

Mobile Floor Company is a family-run flooring service based in Blackpool, proudly serving homes right across the Fylde Coast. Instead of a traditional showroom, we bring the flooring shop directly to your door.

With carpet, laminate, vinyl, LVT and artificial grass samples to hand, you can see exactly how each option looks in your own home before you decide.

Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT)

Luxury Vinyl Tile is a premium option designed for a flawless, long-lasting finish. Unlike click systems, LVT is fully glued down to a perfectly prepared subfloor, which means the floor needs levelling with precision before fitting. The result is a sleek, hardwearing surface that feels solid underfoot and looks every bit as luxurious as it sounds.

  • Solid, premium feel underfoot
  • Realistic wood and stone looks
  • Hardwearing and long-lasting

Laminate

Laminate is one of the most versatile options on the market: stylish, practical and budget-friendly. With a wide choice of colours and finishes, it gives you the look of real wood without the high price tag or heavy upkeep. It's hard-wearing, easy to clean and can even be fitted on steps, with laminate risers and carpet treads, or fully laminated stairs for a sleek, modern look.

  • Budget-friendly wood looks
  • Hard-wearing and easy to clean
  • Can be fitted on stairs

SPC (Stone Plastic Composite)

SPC flooring is a tough, 100% waterproof option built to last. With a rigid core, it resists dents and warping, making it ideal for kitchens, bathrooms and busy areas. It comes in realistic wood and stone finishes and is easy to maintain. Please note the floor must be completely level before installation.

  • 100% waterproof
  • Resists dents and warping
  • Realistic wood and stone finishes

Engineered Wood

Engineered wood combines the beauty of real wood with enhanced durability. Made from layers of hardwood and plywood, it stays stable through changes in temperature and humidity, so it suits a wide range of rooms. Available in many finishes, it brings real warmth and elegance to your space while being easier to look after than solid wood.

  • Real wood surface
  • More stable than solid wood
  • Warm, natural finish

Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between LVT, laminate and SPC?

LVT is glued down for a solid, premium feel; laminate is a budget-friendly floating floor; SPC has a rigid, 100% waterproof core. We'll help you choose what suits each room and budget.

Does the floor need to be level first?

Yes, LVT and SPC need a level, prepared subfloor for the best finish. We'll assess this at your free measure.

Can you lay herringbone or parquet?

Yes, we fit herringbone and parquet patterns for a striking, high-end look.
